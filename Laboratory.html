    About H-PCN Lab

High-Performance Computing and Networking (H-PCN) Laboratory is part of the Intelligent Monitoring Of Production Safety Key Laboratory. Currently, the H-PCN Lab focuses on Heterogeneous Network, Energy-efficient Wireless Communications, Device-to-Device Communications and Physical Layer Security.
